Prevention Tips

Here are some preventions tips that you should pay attention.

Avoid close contact Avoid close contact with people who are sick, even inside your home. Put distance between yourself and other people outside of your home, remember that, come people without symptons may be able to spread virus.
Cover your mouth , nose and hands You could spread COVID-19 to others even if you do not feel sick Everyone should wear a cloth face cover and gloves when they have to go out in public. Continue to keep distance between yourself and others. The cloth face and gloves are not a substitute for a social distancing.
Know how it spreads There is currently no vaccine to prevent coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19). The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus .
Clean and disinfect Clean and desinfect frequently touched surfaces daily. This includes tables, light switches , desks , phones , keyboards and toilets. Then, use a household desinfectant.
